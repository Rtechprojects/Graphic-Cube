About

Here at Graphic Cube we can assist you in all your design needs from Logo’s and Business cards to Page layouts and Banners. We also do CV’s and Resume’s to Website and App designs. Basically, if you need it designed we can offer you that service.

How we do things?

Step one; will be a briefing of the customer to get a better understanding of what your needs are and your idea or vision of the design in question. Step Two; will be the drawing and mockup of the work you need done. Strep Three; will be the final design and handover after full payment.

Pricing?

We are very well priced for the services we deliver and a quick estimate can be done on all your reqeusts.

What programs do we use?

We use all adobe programs to do our designs, mockups and prototypes.
